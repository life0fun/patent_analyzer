# Patent Claim Comparison Report

## Similarities
- Both claims involve receiving a signal to determine a target or destination.
- Both claims involve controlling a propulsion system to move the UAV/drone.

## Differences
- **Signal Source:**
  - Claim A: Remote controller
  - Claim B: User device

- **Target/Destination Identification:**
  - Claim A: Identifies a target location
  - Claim B: Determines a destination

- **Path Calculation:**
  - Claim A: Calculates a flight path
  - Claim B: Computes an alternative route after obstacle detection

- **Obstacle Detection:**
  - Claim A: No mention of obstacle detection
  - Claim B: Detects an obstacle in a projected path

- **Actuation/Control:**
  - Claim A: Actuates a motor
  - Claim B: Controls a propulsion system
